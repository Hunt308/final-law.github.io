Attorney & Staff profiles

Melissa Sarris Kauttu

After graduating from Pepperdine University School of Law in 1989, Melissa practiced at the law firm of Risley and Associates in Costa Mesa, California, representing the business, real estate, estate planning, and probate interests of high net worth clients in the Southern California area. Melissa took that practice into the law firm Melissa S. Kauttu, Attorney at Law, in Santa Ana, California, and maintained her practice in Southern California until 2001, before relocating to her family’s hometown of St.Augustine, Florida.

Melissa went on to become an Assistant State Attorney for the Seventh Judicial Circuit in St.Johns County prosecuting all levels of crimes and serving as a Drug Court prosecutor before leaving the State in 2009. Melissa also served as Regional Conflict Counsel for the Office of Criminal Conflict and Regional Civil Counsel from 2009-2011 and the Judicial Administration Commission for all level of felony offenses as appointed by the Circuit Court from 2011 and extending into 2016.

On January 1st, 2014, she opened and founded The Law Station, a dba of Melissa Sarris Kauttu, P.A., expanding her network of service to the North Florida community in general practice areas, with emphasis in Business, Estate Planning and Probate, and later adding Mediation, which is non-attorney function.

Melissa can mediate any type of case and is presently certified by the Florida Supreme Court in Family Law and has a pending certification in Circuit Civil. She can mediate matters prior to or after the filng of an action and can mediate cases seeking modifications of judgments from earlier litigation between the parties. If during active litigation, she will seek a court order and give notice of mediation to all parties.

She is currently an active member of the Florida Bar, St. Johns County Bar Association, Putnam County Bar Association, Florida Women’s Lawyer’s Association, and the Collaborative Family Law Group of Northeast Florida

Debbie Martin, Legal assistant

Debbie brings more than 40+ years of experience to the Law Station as a first class legal assistant. She began her career in the legal field working as a floating Judicial Assistant to many county and Circuit Court judges in Duval County. Since leaving the courthouse she has worked for attorneys in the private sector, and although Debbie has experience in criminal, personal injury, workers compensation, employment litigation, civil litigation and probate, she has spent the majority of her career specializing in family law.
